FAQ
Common questions answered.
Is CentAI a licensed financial advisor?

No — CentAI is an AI-powered tool that provides financial information and guidance. It is not a licensed financial advisor and its responses should not be treated as regulated financial advice. For major financial decisions, please consult a qualified professional.

How accurate is the financial data?

CentAI uses real-time web search to pull the latest market data, stock prices, and financial news. General financial guidance is based on widely accepted principles and best practices.

Is my financial data safe?

Yes. Your conversations are stored securely using Supabase, a trusted database platform. We never sell your data to third parties. You can delete your conversation history at any time.

What is the difference between the plans?

The Free plan gives you 20 questions per month to explore CentAI at no cost — no credit card required. Pro (€14.99/mo) gives you unlimited questions, file uploads, and conversation memory. Elite (€39.99/mo) adds team seats, business KPI analysis, and priority support.

Can I use CentAI on my phone?

Yes! CentAI works on any device. On iPhone, open it in Safari and tap "Add to Home Screen" for a full app experience. On Android, use Chrome and tap "Add to Home Screen".

Can businesses use CentAI?

Absolutely. CentAI is built for both individuals and businesses. It can analyse cash flow, calculate break-even points, track KPIs, and provide CFO-level insight at a fraction of the cost. We also offer white-label licensing for businesses who want to offer CentAI under their own brand.
